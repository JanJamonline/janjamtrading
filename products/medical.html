Medical Equipment Sourcing & Trade Support

Janjam Trading supports the sourcing and international trade of medical equipment and healthcare products for buyers across Europe and Africa. We operate as an independent trade intermediary, connecting hospitals, distributors, government buyers, and healthcare organizations with verified manufacturers and exporters.

Our role is focused on facilitating reliable supply chains, coordinating export-ready documentation, and supporting buyers throughout the sourcing process. We work exclusively on B2B and institutional transactions and do not engage in retail or direct-to-consumer sales.

Through our supplier network, we assist buyers in identifying suitable manufacturers, aligning technical specifications, and ensuring compliance with applicable quality and regulatory requirements.

Product Categories

  • Hospital and clinical equipment
  • Diagnostic and monitoring devices
  • Medical disposables and consumables
  • Surgical instruments and tools
  • Protective and safety equipment
  • Home healthcare products

Forms, Specifications & Supply Scope

  • Single-use and reusable medical products
  • Manual and electronic equipment
  • Standard and customized specifications
  • Bulk supply for institutional and government procurement

Products can be sourced based on buyer-defined specifications, regulatory requirements, and target market standards. Packaging and labeling can be coordinated according to export and destination country needs.

Quality, Compliance & Documentation

We assist buyers in coordinating quality expectations, certifications, and export documentation. This includes support with product specifications, test reports, compliance references, and communication with manufacturers to ensure transparency and alignment.

All sourcing is conducted through verified exporters and manufacturers capable of meeting international trade and regulatory standards applicable to medical and healthcare products.

Who We Work With

  • Hospitals and healthcare institutions
  • Medical distributors and importers
  • Government and public procurement bodies
  • NGOs and humanitarian organizations
  • Private healthcare providers

How Janjam Trading Adds Value

Janjam Trading acts as a single point of coordination between buyers and suppliers. We support supplier identification, technical communication, documentation alignment, sample handling where applicable, and ongoing coordination throughout the sourcing and export process.
