Indian Spices Sourcing & Trade Support

Janjam Trading facilitates the sourcing and international trade of high-quality Indian spices for buyers across Europe and Africa. We act as an independent trade intermediary, connecting importers, distributors, and food industry buyers with reliable spice manufacturers and exporters in India.

India is one of the world’s leading producers of spices, offering a wide range of varieties known for their aroma, purity, and consistency. Through our supplier network, we support buyers in identifying suitable exporters, aligning commercial terms, and coordinating quality and shipment requirements.

Our focus is on bulk and B2B transactions. We do not engage in retail sales. All sourcing is handled through verified exporters and processors capable of meeting international trade standards.
