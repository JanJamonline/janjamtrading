Semi-Precious Gemstone Beads Sourcing & Trade Support

Janjam Trading facilitates the sourcing and international trade of semi-precious gemstone beads for buyers across Europe and Africa. We act as an independent trade intermediary, connecting importers, wholesalers, jewelry manufacturers, and distributors with reliable gemstone processors and bead manufacturers.

Our focus is on bulk and B2B transactions. We work with established suppliers capable of producing consistent quality, standardized bead sizing, and export-ready packaging suitable for international trade.

All sourcing is conducted through verified exporters experienced in handling semi-precious gemstones for jewelry manufacturing, fashion accessories, and decorative applications.

Quality, Grading & Export Handling

We assist buyers in aligning quality expectations, grading parameters, and lot consistency with suppliers. Our role includes facilitating communication regarding material authenticity, processing methods, and export handling requirements.

All gemstone beads are sourced through exporters familiar with international trade documentation, packing standards, and destination market requirements.
