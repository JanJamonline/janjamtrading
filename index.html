About Janjam Trading

Janjam Trading is an independent international trade brokerage and sourcing firm dedicated to connecting buyers across Europe, Africa, and the Middle East with reliable manufacturers, exporters, and producers based in India.

We operate as a professional intermediary, bridging the gap between international buyers and Indian suppliers by managing communication, coordination, and commercial alignment throughout the sourcing and procurement process.

Our role is to simplify cross-border trade for our clients. We assist businesses in identifying suitable suppliers, validating product capabilities, coordinating pricing structures, documentation, and logistics requirements, and ensuring smooth and transparent information flow between all parties involved.

Janjam Trading focuses exclusively on B2B and bulk trade. We work with importers, distributors, wholesalers, retailers, project buyers, and industrial clients who require dependable sourcing partners and long-term trade relationships.

By maintaining strong supplier networks in India and a clear understanding of buyer requirements in international markets, we aim to create transparent, efficient, and sustainable trade partnerships built on trust, consistency, and professional execution.
